SERVICES

Mobile Pantry

The Connecticut Food Bank has a mobile pantry which goes around the state and provides free food to anyone who is in need. Keep up to date with our social media to find updates and upcoming adjustments to our mobile pantry schedule.

Commodity Supplemenetal Food Program

The Connecticut Food Bank provides a supplimental food resource to improve the health of low-income senior citizens which meet federal income guidelines through the CSFP. This program supplenments its members' diets with a plethora of nutritious foods.

Volunteering Opportunity

The Conneticut Food Bank welcomes all individuals who want to help. There are lots of options to choose from, whether it be working in the warehouse, office, or at various other sites that we employ. Contact us for more info.
